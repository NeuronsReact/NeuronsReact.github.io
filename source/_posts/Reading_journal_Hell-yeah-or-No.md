## Updating identity
#### 1
Topic: Think of what to do if you are completely satiated, even with relaxing. 想想不再需要更多钱、关注度，甚至不需要再多的休闲的时候，你会做什么。

#### 2
1 concept.

Focus your time on your local community: Spend time on meeting the people who likely have close relationships with you.

Focus your time on your global community: Spend time on the rest of the world.

Both of they have the benefits and drawbacks. You can choose the balance.

将时间投入给身边人，还是身边人以外有关联的人？每个人有自己的平衡。

#### 3
1 concept and 2 actions.

Concept: No matter what you say , your actions reveal what you actually want.

Action 1: Stop lying yourself and admit your priorities.

Action 2: start doing what you say and you want to do, and see if it's really true.

你做了什么决定你的真实愿望，不是你说了什么，也不是你认为自己想做什么、要做什么。

#### 4
Truth: Keep earning your titles, or it expires. 如果不能一直获得titles，那么它们终将消失。
